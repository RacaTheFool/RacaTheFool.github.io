Our Mission

Our mission is to help people realize that they have purpose, and that they can show others they have purpose too! It's not about the money, it's about each individual. We only charge money because we have to be able to sustain ourselves. Seriously, it's not a greed thing.

Anyways, we want to make the world a better place, and we plan to do that by helping others recognize their self worth. We are open to suggestions and would invite anyone who has a suggestion on what other services we could provide, or any improvements to current services, to email us at fafbcustomerservice@friendsandfistbumps.com.
